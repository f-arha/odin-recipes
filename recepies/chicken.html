Fried Chicken

Description

This fried chicken recipe is crispy, golden brown, and full of flavor. A simple yet delicious way to enjoy tender chicken, perfect for any meal. The crackers provide the perfect crunch, while the seasoned flour and potato flakes create a flavorful coating.

Ingredients

  • 30 saltine crackers
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons dry potato flakes
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 cups vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Place crackers in a large resealable plastic bag; seal bag and crush crackers with a rolling pin until they are coarse crumbs.
  2. Add the flour, potato flakes, seasoned salt, and pepper to the bag and mix well.
  3. Beat the egg in a shallow dish or bowl.
  4. One by one, dredge the chicken pieces in the egg, then place them in the bag with the crumb mixture. Seal the bag and shake to coat.
  5. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350°F (175°C).
  6. Fry the chicken, turning frequently, until golden brown and juices run clear, about 15 to 20 minutes.
